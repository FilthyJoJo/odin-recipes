Omelette Tacos

An Omelette Taco

The Omelette Taco is a simple and easy recipe to learn and can be made with few ingredients.

It can be incredibly filling, calorie friendly, and incredibly delicious with just how much of a blank canvas the combination of eggs and a tortilla wrap provides.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Tortilla Wrap
  • Olive Oil or Alternative Such as Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Vegetable or Meat

Steps

  1. Preheat two pans, one for the veggies, meat, and eggs and another for the tortilla wraps
  2. Add olive oil into the pan and fry vegetables or meat to personal taste.
  3. While frying, beat a minimum of 1 egg, ideally 2, in a bowl and add salt and pepper.
  4. Add eggs to frying ingredients and allow eggs to cover as much of the surface of the pan as possible.
  5. On the other pan, place tortilla wrap and flip occasionally as the omelette cooks.
  6. Once omelette is finished, place atop the tortilla wrap and close, finish cooking tortilla wrap to preferred doneness.
  7. Immediately begin from step 2 again if making more than one omelette taco.
  8. Serve on a plate and enjoy with any condiment or sauce you like.
