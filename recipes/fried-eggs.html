Fried Eggs

The fried egg is one of the simplest foods to cook for breakfast or any other occasion.

It's a good food that can added onto many other dishes or spiced up to be enjoyed by itself.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil or alternative such as butter

Steps

  1. Add a tablespoon of olive oil into your pan and heat up on medium heat.
  2. Wait until oil begins to shimmer or loses viscocity and then crack in an egg.
  3. Add salt and pepper to the egg and allow the egg to cook until the egg whites become completely opaque.
  4. Optional: use a lid and a small splash of water to more quickly cook the top of the egg for a runnier yolk.
  5. Serve egg on a plate and enjoy. Multiple eggs can be cook at the same time, but they will fuse together.
